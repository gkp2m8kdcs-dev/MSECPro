Overview

NHJTOP helps you weigh life-changing decisions — switching jobs, taking a high-upside offer, or sticking with a stable path — by turning stories into numbers.

Model a Stable Path and a High Upside scenario side-by-side, then see expected earnings, volatility, job-loss probability, and burnout risk combined into a single risk-adjusted score.

Offline-only No accounts No tracking No third-party SDKs

Features

Risk-Adjusted Scoring

Stability × Security × Growth × Wellbeing — one number that captures what spreadsheets miss.

Expected Earnings Curves

See year-by-year projections for any scenario over a 1–30 year horizon.

Volatility Lab

Monte Carlo paths and outcome histograms powered by on-device simulations.

Insights Engine

Plain-English summaries, comparisons, and a personalised action plan.

Custom Scenarios

Save and edit scenarios locally. Compare any two in the Dashboard.

Time & Energy Allocation

A pie chart view of how each scenario spends your time and energy.

Optional Cover Photo

Pick or capture a cover image — stored locally on your device only.

Customisable Insights

Favourites, expand states, swipe actions, and reset controls.

Screens

  • Dashboard — Comparative risk-adjusted bar chart and time/energy pie chart.
  • Risk Calculator — Inputs for base salary, growth, volatility, job-loss probability, severance, and burnout risk; save scenarios locally.
  • Volatility Lab — Monte Carlo path line chart with interactive sliders and an outcome histogram.
  • Insights — Analysis table with clear, novel framing and a tailored action plan.
  • Saved Scenarios — Quick access to every scenario you have created, with swipe actions.
  • Settings — Privacy & disclaimer, preferences, optional cover photo, and reset controls.

Design

Privacy

NHJTOP does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no analytics SDK, no advertising identifier, and no third-party data processor. Saved scenarios, preferences, and an optional cover photo are stored locally inside the iOS sandbox.

Read the full Privacy Policy and Terms of Use.

Compliance

  • Offline-only — the app makes no outbound network requests.
  • No third-party chart libraries — all visualisations use Core Graphics.
  • Disclaimer and privacy statements are surfaced in-app on first use and in Settings → Privacy & Disclaimer.
  • High-contrast colours, scalable text, and Dynamic Type support for accessibility.
