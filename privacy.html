Privacy Policy

NHJTOP — Career Risk Dashboard for iOS

Effective date: 2026-07-18 · Offline by design · No accounts · No tracking

Summary

NHJTOP does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. The scenarios you save and the cover photo you pick stay on your device. Nothing is uploaded, and nothing is shared with anyone.

1. Data Collection

This app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. There is no account, login, analytics SDK, tracking pixel, advertising identifier, or third-party data processor integrated into the app.

2. On-Device Storage

All saved scenarios, user preferences, and an optional cover photo are stored locally on your device inside the iOS app sandbox (via UserDefaults and the app's documents directory). Nothing is uploaded to a server, and no data leaves your device under any circumstance.

3. Photos & Camera

The Photos and Camera features are entirely optional. They are only used to let you pick or capture a custom cover image for the dashboard, which is then saved locally to the app's sandbox. The app does not access your photo library beyond the single image you select, and the image is never transmitted off-device.

The system permission strings used by the app are:

  • NSPhotoLibraryUsageDescription — "Select a cover photo for your dashboard. Photos stay on-device and are not uploaded."
  • NSCameraUsageDescription — "Take a photo to use as a cover image in the app. Photos stay on-device and are not uploaded."
  • NSPhotoLibraryAddUsageDescription — "Save exported charts and screenshots to your Photos library."

4. Network Access

The app works fully offline. No outbound network requests are made for analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, content delivery, or any other purpose. NSAllowsArbitraryLoads is set to false in Info.plist.

5. Your Controls

You can remove saved scenarios, reset insights favourites and expand states, or remove the cover photo at any time from Settings. Uninstalling the app deletes all locally stored data.

6. Children's Privacy

NHJTOP is intended for general audiences and does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the revised version will be posted at this URL and the "Effective date" above will be refreshed. Because the app does not network-connect, no in-app notification of changes is possible — please revisit this page periodically.
